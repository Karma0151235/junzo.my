SAP S/4HANA Sales - Functions and Innovations

Overview

In this course, participants familiar with Sales in SAP ERP 6.0 will be introduced to the feautures and functionality provided by SAP S/4HANA in this area. Analytical features for Sales in SAP S/4HANA are also discussed.

Goals

This course will prepare you to:

  • Understand, explain and work with SAP S/4HANA Sales
  • Describe the changes in billing functionality compared to SAP ERP
  • Execute order-to-cash processes in SAP S/4HANA using the SAP Fiori Launchpad
  • Work with the analytical features in SAP S/4HANA Sales
