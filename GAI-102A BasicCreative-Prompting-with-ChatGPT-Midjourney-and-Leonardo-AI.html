GAI-102A Basic Creative Prompting with ChatGPT Midjourney and Leonardo AI

WHAT YOU WILL LEARN

By the end of this workshop, participants will:

  • Understand the underlying principles of how ChatGPT interprets and responds to prompts
  • Be able to craft effective prompts for a wide range of use cases including creative prompting
  • Experience the creative prompting using ChatGPT, Midjourney and Leonardo AI
  • Explore the basics and comparing Midjourney with Leonardo AI

    • AUDIENCE

    Anyone with basic understanding of AI, either technical or non-technical background.

    PREREQUISITES

    Before attending this course, students must have:

  • Basic computer skills and internet literacy
  • Familiarity with the concept of artificial intelligence is advantageous but not mandatory
  • No previous experience with ChatGPT or any other AI models is required

    • COURSE OBJECTIVES

  • To introduce participants to the basics of ChatGPT, including its capabilities and limitations
  • To provide participants with a strong understanding of the basics of prompting ChatGPT
  • To give participants hands-on experience with crafting effective prompts in a variety of scenarios
  • To guide participants on crafting creative prompts in ChatGPT
  • To explore the fundamental usage of Midjourney and Leonardo AI
  • To compare the pros and cons of Midjourney and Leonardo AI

    • GAI-102A Basic Creative Prompting with ChatGPT Midjourney and Leonardo AI

    COURSE CONTENTS

    Module 1: Introduction to the Workshop

  • Welcome and introductions, course objectives, agenda for the day, and housekeeping notes

    • Module 2: Overview of ChatGPT

  • Explanation of AI and language models, what ChatGPT is, how it works, and how it interacts with prompts
  • Understanding the capabilities and limitations of ChatGPT

    • Module 3: Basics of Prompting

  • An overview of what prompts are and how they guide the output of ChatGPT
  • Explanation of key principles in creating effective prompts, including clarity, specificity, and tone

    • Module 4: Exploring Creative Prompting using ChatGPT

  • Exploration of what are creative prompts and the limits of that in ChatGPT Free plan
  • To introduce ChatGPT Plus plan with plugins which turns on the creative side of ChatGPT

    • Module 5: Introduction to Midjourney & Leonardo AI

  • Exploring how to create an account for both Midjourney and Leonardo AI
  • Navigating and familiarizing the features and options in Midjourney and Leonardo AI, as well as defining the pros and cons between Midjourney and Leonardo AI
