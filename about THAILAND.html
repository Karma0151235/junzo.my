Our Company

Junzo Sdn Bhd was incorporated in June 2011. We are an innovative technology and training provider in Malaysia, we work together with our partners in multiple continent to give you the best of solution together with upskill roadmap through our wide experience and professionalism. Besides, we also focus on professional IT training and Education development training. We offer a range of training development programs that can be broadly categorized into IT professional certification and academic programs.

Vision, Mission & Objective

Vision

To become a worldwide leader in IT transformation through solutions and training. To exceed client expectations. To establish a work-life balance and user-friendly atmosphere for employees by prioritising Junzo values such as Employee First, Trust, Transparency, Flexibility, and Value Centricity.

Mission

Conduct the business with a high level of ethics and honesty. To earn client trust through the delivery of exceptional services, support, and ongoing enhancements. Appreciate the high-performing employee and consistently provide them with training in cutting-edge technologies. Emphasizes transparency and open communication at all levels to eradicate hierarchical leadership.

Objective

Value the high-performance employees and continues to invest in upskill employees with the latest technologies. Practices transparency and open communication at all level to eliminate hierarchy leadership and encourage employees to participate in innovation.
