National Human Capital Conference & Exhibition (NHCCE) 2024, Kuala Lumpur

We are excited to announce our participation in the National Human Capital Conference & Exhibition (NHCCE) 2024, taking place from September 30 to October 2, 2024. This premier event brings together thought leaders, industry experts, and practitioners in the field of human capital to discuss the latest trends, challenges, and innovations.

Join us at NHCCE 2024 to explore cutting-edge strategies and solutions that can transform your organization's human capital management. We look forward to engaging with fellow professionals and contributing to the dynamic exchange of ideas and best practices.

Mark your calendars and see you at MITEC!
