National Human Capital Conference & Exhibition (NHCCE) 2023 Northern Region at Setia SPICE Convention Centre, Penang

As Junzo Sdn Bhd, we have participated in the Northern Region of the National Human Capital Conference and Exhibition (NHCCE) in 2023 as an exhibitor. The event was held at Penang's Setia SPICE Convention Centre from May 31 to June 1, 2023.

The conference's theme, "HR 5.0: Evolution in Human Resource Development," aims to equip executives, HR practitioners, working professionals, and industry participants with the knowledge and skills necessary to lead their own organisations' human capital transformations.

At Junzo Sdn Bhd, we are excited to introduce our cutting-edge learning management system and are looking forward to this special event. We believe that organisations may greatly benefit from our platform as they work to improve their human resources.
