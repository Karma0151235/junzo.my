Basic Transportation Management in SAP S/4HANA

Overview

This course will help you to prepare for the blueprinting stage of your SAP S/4HANA logistics execution implementation based on the (basic) transportation management functional scope. It will enable you to use the system in your role as a business analyst or business user. You will gain an overview of the key business transactions and system capabilities to enable you to design the processes you will use within your own organization. You will learn how the (basic) transportation management process in SAP S/4HANA can help you to support the order fulfillment process, transportation planning, subcontracting, execution, charge calculation and settlement. A self-paced e-learning version of this course (S4611E) is also available with an active subscription to the SAP Learning Hub (Professional or IoT and Digital Supply Chain solution edition).

Goals

This course will prepare you to:

  • Outline transportation management business processes in SAP S/4HANA
  • Identify and examine the building blocks necessary for the configuration of a basic transportation management process in SAP S/4HANA.
  • Engage in transportation network maintenance, transportation requirement creation, transportation planning, shipment execution, freight charge calculation, settlement, and freight cost distribution.
  • Use SAP S/4HANA using the Fiori UX
