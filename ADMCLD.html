Introduction to SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP) Administration

Overview

This course provides participants with an overview of administrative tasks in the SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP). It covers the concepts of the intelligent, sustainable enterprise followed by the architecture of the SAP BTP.The architecture of global accounts, subaccounts, services, runtime environments are shown like Cloud Foundry, Kyma (Kubernetes), and ABAP.
