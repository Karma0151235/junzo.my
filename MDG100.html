SAP Master Data Governance on SAP S/4HANA

Overview

This course provides you the business knowledge you need to use SAP Master Data Governance to ensure ongoing master data quality. This course comes with practical hands-ons to deepen the theoretical lessons.

Goals

This course will prepare you to:

  • Present the MDG solution strategy of SAP
  • Explain the data domains and processes related to MDG
  • Explain the business processes used within MDG

SAP Master Data Governance on SAP S/4HANA

Content

  • Introduction to SAP Master Data Governance
  • MDG for Material
  • MDG for Business Partner, Supplier, Customer
  • MDG for Financials and Hierarchy Management
  • MDG Mass Changes and Multiple Object Processing
  • MDG Mass Processing and Consolidation
  • Process Analytics in SAP MDG
  • MDG – Data Quality Management

Audience

  • Application Consultant
  • Business Analyst
  • Business Process Architect
  • Business Process Owner / Team Lead / Power User
  • Solution Architect

SAP Master Data Governance on SAP S/4HANA

Prerequisites

Essential

  • Experience in SAP Business Suite ERP or SAP S/4HANA
  • Experience with SAP ERP master data for financial, material or business partner/customer/supplier master data

Recommended

  • Handling SAP Fiori launchpad
  • Business Workflow Floor Plan Manager (FPM) for Web Dynpro ABAP
  • Application Link Enabling (ALE) Technology
